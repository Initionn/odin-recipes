Pasta! An italian pastime.

Description:

In this recipe, you will use flour, eggs, oil, salt, and water, and together those will create one of the most amazing pastas you've ever had.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Knead the dough.
  2. Turn the dough and knead for 10 minutes
  3. Let rest 10 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough in to balls.
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough out into desired shapes.

And you're all done! Congratulations!
