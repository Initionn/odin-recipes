Pizza!

Description:

An incredible pepperoni pizza you can't wait to enjoy.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps:

  1. Form dough out into pizza shell
  2. Put sauce down
  3. Put cheese over sauce
  4. Apply toppings of your choice
  5. Cook in oven until done!

And you're done! Congratulations!
